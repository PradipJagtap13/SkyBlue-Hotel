Our Story

Founded in 2010, SkyBlue Hotel began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where the sky meets the hills. Located at the highest point in Lonavala, we provide our guests with an unparalleled view of the Western Ghats.


Whether you are here for a weekend getaway, a romantic honeymoon, or a corporate retreat, our mission is to provide personalized service that makes you feel right at home.
